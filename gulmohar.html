Gulmohar (Flame Tree)

Care Instructions:

  • Prefers full sunlight.
  • Requires well-drained sandy or loamy soil.
  • Water young trees regularly; mature ones need less care.
  • Prune to maintain shape and prevent branch breakage.

Ecological Benefits:

  • Provides excellent shade in hot climates.
  • Supports biodiversity by attracting birds and insects.
  • Improves soil fertility with nitrogen-fixing roots.
  • Acts as an ornamental tree enhancing landscapes.
