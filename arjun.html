Arjun (Terminalia arjuna)

Care Instructions

Arjun tree prefers moist, fertile soil and grows well near rivers or water bodies. Requires full sun and regular watering during the early years.

Ecological Benefits

🍃 Bark is used in Ayurvedic medicine for heart health.

🌱 Provides shade and improves air quality.

🌳 Strengthens soil near riverbanks and prevents erosion.
