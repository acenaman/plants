Insulin Plant

Care Instructions

Prefers partial shade and moist soil. Requires regular watering. Grows well in pots and garden beds.

Ecological Benefits

  • Leaves are traditionally used to manage diabetes.
  • Acts as ground cover, reducing soil erosion.
  • Adds greenery to home gardens.
